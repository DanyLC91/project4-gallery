About Us

Hawthorne Fine Art Gallery is an art institution rooted in the heart of Dublin, where the world of contemporary and modern art comes to life. Founded with a deep-seated love for art, our gallery serves as a platform for artists to showcase their creativity and for art enthusiasts to appreciate their work.


Located on the historic Temple Bar district, Hawthorne Fine Art Gallery is housed in a beautifully restored townhouse. Steeped in local history, this space provides a warm and inviting atmosphere for visitors to explore our carefully curated collection. Our exhibitions feature a wide array of contemporary and modern artworks, offering a glimpse into the ever-evolving art landscape.


In today's interconnected world, we recognize the importance of accessibility. Hawthorne Fine Art Gallery extends its reach beyond our physical location, providing art lovers with an online platform where they can browse, acquire, and enjoy art from the comfort of their homes. This digital presence allows us to share the beauty and significance of art with a global audience.


Join us on a journey through the world of contemporary and modern art. Whether you're an experienced collector, a burgeoning artist, or simply someone who appreciates the power of artistic expression, Hawthorne Fine Art Gallery welcomes you to explore, connect, and be inspired. Experience the diverse and dynamic art world through our gallery's physical presence and online platform. Hawthorne Fine Art Gallery is not just a place; it's a celebration of creativity and a hub for the art community.
